Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this project?

This project aims to study how speech patterns vary across different regions and how voices may reflect geographic origins. Your contribution helps in building a speech-based research dataset.

How will my voice be used?

Your voice recording will be used for research and educational purposes to train an AI voice model by analyzing regional speech characteristics. All data will be anonymized and securely stored.
